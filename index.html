NBA Salary Cap Explorer

The NBA Salary Cap Explorer allows you to explore every single salary in the NBA by team and player. Every column is a team, and every block inside a column is an individual player. Scroll Over blocks to explore the player and contract further. Switch between years to explore how players contract's shrink or grown from year to year, and explore a teams total salaries in proportion to the Salary Cap.
